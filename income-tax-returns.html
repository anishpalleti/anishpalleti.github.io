What Are Income Tax Returns?

An income tax return reports your income, deductions, credits, and tax liability to the IRS. Individuals typically file Form 1040.

What to report

  • Wages, salary, and tips (Form W-2).
  • Self-employment income (Schedule C).
  • Interest, dividends, and other income.

Filing and deadlines

Most individuals file by the annual deadline (usually April). Extensions may be available for filing, but taxes owed are still due on time.
